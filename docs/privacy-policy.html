Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Overview

Shogi Do ("the App") is a standalone shogi (Japanese chess) game that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertisements.

Network Access

The App does not connect to the internet. All gameplay, including the AI opponent, runs locally on your device. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. No data is collected from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
